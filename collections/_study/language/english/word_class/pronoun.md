# 4. Interrogative Pronouns

- `person`
  - **subjective**: who
  - **objective**: whom
  - **possessive**: whose
- `thing`
  - what: 불특정
  - which: 단정지음
- `time`
  - when
- `place`
  - where
- `way`
  - how
- `reason`
  - why
